It's a serious Business

We have been providing Chichester with superb offerings since 1990.

Our success is down to a varied and exciting choice, use of only the finest ingredients, and first class service. Our sandwiches are hand made on the permises every day. The breads we use are traditionally baked by an independent baker. Because our bread does not contain preservatives, all sandwiches have a one day shelf life. We bake our own baguettes and speciality rolls on site every morning to ensure absolute freshness.

  • We use only approved reputable suppliers
  • We do not use processed or reconstitued meats
  • Our eggs are free range
  • Our beaf come from the finest Scottish free range herds
  • Our smoked and poached salmon are wild
  • We use a low fat spread and mayonnaise

Sandwiches are available on granary or wholemeal farmhouse and white bloomer.
